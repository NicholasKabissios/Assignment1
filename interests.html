My interests in IT.

An ideal job in IT for me would most likely be a software developer, I have already enjoyed taking on a few personal projects in the past and have yet to feel burnt out or unhappy in the process. More specifically, I have mostly created software for niche purposes. There have been times in group chats with friends and communities that I've been involved with where people bring up a desire for a certain feature or a software, and if I had been free at the time I attempted to fill that desire. My interest in IT was sparked from a young age, using technology frequently, since when I was being raised, the world was recently seeing an uptake in technology in daily life. From there, I got interested in video games, which led me to forums on the internet where I would be influenced by the more tech savvy people I would talk to. In high school I studied IT from Year 9 to my final year, doing Software Development as a 3,4 subject. I got the most out of studying Software Development, more so than any previous subject. I found that Software Development gave me a good overview on IT in general, but also focusing on what I enjoyed the most, which was developing solutions.
